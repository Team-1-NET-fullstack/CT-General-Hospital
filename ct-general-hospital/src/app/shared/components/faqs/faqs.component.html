FAQs

1. What is an online doctor consultation or online medical consultation?

Ans: When you speak to a doctor about your health concerns through an audio/video/ chat, it is known as an online doctor consultation or online medical consultation. This is a good alternative for times when you cannot visit a doctor physically at the clinic or hospital.

2. Do you provide online doctor consultation for emergencies?

Ans: No. We do not recommend or provide online consultations for emergencies.

3. When should you consult a specialist doctor?

Ans: If your health concern is complex and your physician feels that it needs the attention of a specialized doctor, you will be referred to one.

4. Will my e-prescription be valid for offline pharmacies?

Ans: Yes. The e-prescription will be valid for offline pharmacies as well.
